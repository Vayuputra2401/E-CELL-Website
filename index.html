Mentorship

"Mentorship is a crucial aspect of startup success. A mentor can provide guidance, support, and advice to help entrepreneurs navigate the challenges of starting and growing a business. Here are the benefits of having a mentor for your startup: Industry knowledge and experience Networking opportunities Honest feedback and advice Access to resources and support Increased chances of success and growth. Find a mentor who aligns with your vision and values, and work together to bring your startup to the next level.".

Funding

"Entrepreneurship cells often provide funding opportunities for startups as part of their mission to support entrepreneurship. This funding can come in the form of grants, loans, or equity investments. Here are some benefits of funding from an entrepreneurship cell: Access to a network of experienced entrepreneurs and investors Opportunities for mentorship and support Increased visibility and credibility Access to resources and facilities Connections to potential customers and partners.

Competitions and Events

"Events play a crucial role in the ecosystem of an entrepreneurship cell. They provide opportunities for networking, learning, and showcasing entrepreneurial ideas. Here are some benefits of events organized by an entrepreneurship cell: Networking with entrepreneurs, investors, and industry experts Exposure to new ideas and perspectives Opportunities for learning and skill development Platform to showcase and pitch your startup Building community and support for entrepreneurship.

Our Events

We are a team of dedicated students endeavoring to spread the spirit of entrepreneurship through various activities and insightful workshops and guest lectures from various start-ups. Events like Alumni Connect, Interactive speaker sessions, and other related activities are held to keep the aspiring minds grow and cultivate the spirit of job creation. It not only ignites the young minds but also nurtures the ideas of one of the most talented students of India. The vision is to guide the students to take entrepreneurship as a career, as a path to success, and as a journey of wisdom.
